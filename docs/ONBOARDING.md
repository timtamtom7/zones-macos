# Zones — Onboarding

4 screens for the world clock / timezone app.

---

## Screen 1 — Welcome to Zones

**Headline:** Know the time, everywhere.

**Body:** Zones keeps all your cities on one screen — no mental math, no confusion. See current times, day and night, and find the perfect meeting slot across time zones.

**Illustration:** A clean, flat illustration of a stylized Earth globe viewed from above (pole perspective). 3-4 city "pins" dot the globe in different positions, each with a floating time bubble. Subtle latitude/longitude grid lines visible. A day/night gradient sweeps across the globe. Deep indigo and yellow tones.

**CTA Button:** "Add Your First City →"

**Skip link:** "Skip setup" (bottom right)

---

## Screen 2 — Add Your Cities

**Headline:** Which time zones matter to you?

**Body:** Search for any city and add it to your Zones board. Reorder by dragging. Your primary city shows in the menu bar.

**Illustration:** A search bar mockup with "San Francisco" typed in. Below it, 3 floating city result cards (San Francisco, Seattle, Los Angeles) sliding up with a subtle animation. Each card shows the city name, country flag emoji, and current time. Indigo accent color on the selected state.

**CTA Button:** "Search for a City…"

**Secondary link:** "Use my current location"

---

## Screen 3 — Plan Meetings Across Zones

**Headline:** Find time, instantly.

**Body:** Select multiple cities and Zones shows you overlapping business hours — green for everyone available, yellow for partial overlap, red for outside work hours.

**Illustration:** A horizontal timeline strip spanning 3 city rows. A green bar ("9am–6pm overlap") spans across all rows. Small city flag icons on the left. Time labels above. Clear, chart-like, easy to understand at a glance. The green overlap bar is the hero.

**CTA Button:** "Next →"

---

## Screen 4 — You're All Set

**Headline:** Time, organized.

**Body:** Your Zones board is ready. Add cities anytime with `⌘N`. Drag to reorder. Your primary city can show in the menu bar.

**Illustration:** A clean illustration showing the Zones app window with 3 city cards visible in a grid. Each card shows city name, flag emoji, large time, and a day/night arc. A small menu bar icon in the corner showing a time. Minimal, functional, calm. No confetti.

**CTA Button:** "Open Zones"
